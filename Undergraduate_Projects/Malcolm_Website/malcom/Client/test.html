About Malcolm Lismore

Hello, I’m Malcolm Lismore, a freelance photographer based on the rugged North West coast of Scotland. My passion for photography started as a hobby, but over the years, it has grown into a career that brings me immense joy. The natural world is my biggest inspiration, and I feel incredibly lucky to be surrounded by Scotland’s breathtaking landscapes, wildlife, and coastal birds.

As a photographer, my primary focus is capturing the beauty of the outdoors, whether it’s the sweeping hills of the Highlands, the majestic creatures of the wild, or the serene coastal vistas. However, I also enjoy working on a variety of other projects, including weddings, portraits, and special events. I take great pride in creating timeless memories for my clients through my lens.

I believe photography is not just about taking pictures; it’s about telling stories. Every image I capture is a story in itself—an intimate moment frozen in time. Whether you’re looking for a stunning portrait, a wedding album, or some breathtaking landscapes, I am here to help you preserve your special moments in the most beautiful way possible.

Over the years, I have had the privilege of working with many wonderful clients, and I am always excited to take on new projects. If you’re interested in booking me for a wedding, a portrait session, or any other special event, please don’t hesitate to get in touch!

"Photography is the story I fail to put into words." – Malcolm Lismore
